Diffrent States Thali

South indian Thali


A south indian style of lunch or dinner meal, served with a selection of recipes or dishes arranged in plate or thali. in other words, it is a combination of different tastes and flavours organised and served in a single platter.

Punjabi Thali


Punjab is a major producer of wheat, rice and dairy products. The cuisine is heavily influenced by the agriculture and farming lifestyles. Punjabi cooking uses both the traditional and modern methods of cooking. Tandoori style of cooking is traditionally associated with Punjab.

Gujarati Thali


The Gujarati Thali consists of various dishes like ringana methi nu shak (eggplant cooked with fenugreek leaves), Gujarati khatti mithi daal (sweet and sour lentil), undhiyu (mixed vegetable prepared with spinach paste), bhindi sambhariya (ladyfingers curry), aloo rasila (potato and tomato thin curry), steamed basmati rice, badshahi khichdi, Chapati, methi na thepla and bhakhri with kachumber salad along with various types of chutneys.

Rajasthani Thali


This Rajasthani Thali is a delicious thali which is made from pantry staple or cupboard ingredients and features less famous dishes of Rajasthan like Papad ki Kadhi and Mangodi ki sabji. Rajasthan is a state in north India and the land of kings.
